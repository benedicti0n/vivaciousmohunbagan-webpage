IMMORTAL XI

When our country was surrounded in the trap of Britishers, the Mohun Bagan Club defeated East Yorkshire Regiment despite playing barefoot and facing a lots of sledging ,emotional and physical hurdles. Those eleven players became immortal in the hearts of Indians and are even now worshipped by Mohunbagan Fans. A spirit of nationalism sparked throughout the country. The people were then motivated to drive away the British rule.

Mohun Bagan Ratna

If you are a Mohun Bagan fan, then 29th of July is one of the most important days in the calendar for you and no matter how busy you are, you will try your best to take out time and visit the holy club of ours.

29/07/1911 is no ordinary date. It was a day when time stood still as Abhilash Ghosh dived to score the winning header against the East Yorkshire Regiment. 11 men, 10 of whom played barefoot punched above their weight to take the British mojo to the cleaners. The fighting spirit shown by our team caused tremors in the hearts of the British Empire and is considered as a catalyst behind the oppressive rulers taking a step back and shifting the capital to Delhi as the self-belief which that win gave the people of Bengal was too hot to handle for them.

You may be a supporter of some other club but if you are a true patriot, you will stand up and salute the efforts of those 11 men who stood tall amidst torture aplenty to their near and dear ones every other day.

To commemorate the occasion this day is celebrated every year by Mohun Bagan Athletic Club as “The Mohun Bagan Day” when legends of this great club are felicitated along with the best athletes who throughout the year, bring laurels to the club which is regarded as a Temple by the fans.

The Mohun Bagan Ratna is bestowed upon an ex-player who through his game has given the club numerous occasions of joy and pride over the years.
