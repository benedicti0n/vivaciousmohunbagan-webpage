IMMORTAL XI

When our country was surrounded in the trap of Britishers, the Mohun Bagan Club defeated East Yorkshire Regiment despite playing barefoot and facing a lots of sledging ,emotional and physical hurdles. Those eleven players became immortal in the hearts of Indians and are even now worshipped by Mohunbagan Fans. A spirit of nationalism sparked throughout the country. The people were then motivated to drive away the British rule.
